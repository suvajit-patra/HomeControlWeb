Details

Home Control is an android application that helps you to make your own IoT project. It gives you graphical interface to see and change the values of your own firebase database variables.

Features

  • Well Coded

    Made in Android Studio and exceptions are well handled

  • Share Options

    Share your database access with your fiamily members with QR Code or online (Email, Whatsapp etc.)

  • Encrypted Data

    Youe database details is always encrypted
